## Confirmed HR/Recruitment Services

- **Permanent Placement:** Sourcing candidates for long-term roles across various industries.
- **HR Consulting:** Advisory services for organizational structure and talent retention.

## Employer Process
Employers looking to hire staff through Headhunters.lk can expect the following process:
1. **Request Staff:** Employers can submit their requirements via our website's "Request Staff" form or contact us directly.
2. **Consultation:** Our team will review the requirements and consult with you to define the ideal candidate profile.
3. **Sourcing & Screening:** We actively source candidates from our extensive database and network, conducting initial screenings and interviews.
4. **Shortlisting:** A curated list of the best-fit candidates is presented for your review.
5. **Interview & Placement:** We coordinate interviews between you and the candidates and assist with final placement.

### Recruitment Fee and Guarantee Terms
- **Fee:** Our standard recruitment fee is typically based on a percentage of the candidate's first-year annual salary. Specific rates are negotiated based on the volume and exclusivity of the roles.
- **Guarantee:** We provide a standard replacement guarantee if a placed candidate leaves within the first 3 months (90 days) of employment, subject to terms and conditions.

## Candidate Process (Registration & Application)
Job seekers looking for opportunities can expect the following process:
1. **CV Registration:** Candidates can upload their CV and register their details securely on our website.
2. **Profile Review:** Our recruiters review submitted CVs and match them against active vacancies.
3. **Job Application:** Candidates can browse active jobs on our "Vacancies" page and apply directly online.
4. **Interview Preparation:** Shortlisted candidates will be contacted by our team for an initial screening and provided with guidance before client interviews.
5. **Placement:** We support candidates through the offer negotiation process and onboarding.

## Public FAQs

**For Employers:**
- *How long does it take to find a candidate?* Depending on the role's complexity, we typically provide an initial shortlist within 1 to 2 weeks.
- *Do you handle mass recruitment?* Yes, we have the capacity to handle volume hiring for specific projects or expansions.

**For Candidates:**
- *Do I have to pay a fee to register or apply for jobs?* No, our services are completely free for job seekers. We do not charge candidates any placement fees.
- *How can I update my CV?* You can submit a new CV through the website registration form, and our system will update your profile.

## Privacy and Job-Scam Guidance
- **Privacy Policy:** We are committed to protecting your personal data. Your CV and details will never be shared with employers without your explicit consent.
- **Job Scam Awareness:** Headhunters.lk will **never** ask candidates for money, bank details, or payments in exchange for a job offer. All our official communications will come from `@headhunters.lk` email addresses or our official phone numbers. If you suspect a scam, please contact us immediately.
